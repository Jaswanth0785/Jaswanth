Mutton Biryani

Mutton Biryani is a delicious rice dish made with tender pieces of mutton (goat or lamb), basmati rice, and a blend of spices. The mutton is usually marinated with yogurt and spices, then cooked and layered with rice, fried onions, and herbs like mint and coriander. It's cooked together until the flavors mix well. Mutton biryani is rich, aromatic, and enjoyed in many parts of South Asia.
