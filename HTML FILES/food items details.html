Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a popular and flavorful rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic basmati rice, and a mix of spices. It's cooked in layers, often with fried onions, yogurt, and herbs like mint and coriander. Originating from South Asia, it's known for its rich taste and is enjoyed in many variations across India, Pakistan, and other regions.
